Drag & Drop Scheduling

Milestones

Baseline & Slip Tracking
Snapshot your original schedule. Ghost bars and slip badges show exactly where you've drifted.

Role-Based Access
PM and member roles with password protection. Members can update progress and request date changes.

PDF & Excel Export
Print-ready PDF exports with critical path indicators. Excel exports include baselines, notes, and audit data.

Project Log
A shared running log for the whole team. PM messages are visually distinguished from member entries.

PM & Member Inboxes
On login, PMs and members each get a personal inbox — new notes, completed tasks, pending approvals, and resolved date requests, all in one place. See what changed without digging through the chart.
📊
Status Dashboard
A one-screen read on project health — total tasks, completion rate, schedule health, milestone status, and pending approvals at a glance. Know exactly where things stand without scrolling the chart.

Full Audit Trail
Every action timestamped and attributed — date changes, approvals, completions, even failed logins. A complete, PM-only record of who did what, and when.
📅
Workday Calendar
Schedule around real working days — turn weekends off and mark your own holidays and non-working days. Durations and dependency cascades respect your calendar, not just the raw dates.

Works Everywhere
A self-hosted, offline Gantt chart — open and save .gantt files on local drives, network shares, SharePoint, or OneDrive. No server required.

FAQ

Common questions

Does it really work without the internet?
Yes. GanttOWN is a native desktop Gantt chart app that works completely offline once installed. Your project files are saved locally — no cloud sync, no account required after activation.
What does "one-time purchase" mean exactly?
You pay once and own the software. There are no monthly fees or renewal charges. Solo includes 12 months of updates. Team and Business receive all future feature updates as they're released — no additional cost.
Can my team collaborate on the same file?
Yes. Save your .gantt file to a shared network drive, SharePoint, or OneDrive. Team members open it directly — no server setup required. File locking prevents simultaneous edits.
What's the difference between the tiers?
The tiers differ in both team size and collaboration features. Solo supports up to 5 members and is ideal for individual PMs. Team (up to 20) and Business (up to 150) unlock the full collaboration suite — PM and member roles with separate passwords, member inboxes, date change request and approval workflows, PM-to-member messaging, project log, and a full audit trail.
Mac or Windows?
Mac is available now. Windows is in active development — join the waitlist and we'll email you the day it ships. Your license key will work on either platform, so buying today covers you for both.
